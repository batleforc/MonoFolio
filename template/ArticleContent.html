LaserCat: a privacy-preserving smart contract language

Maxime Leriche, 2022

We present a new smart contract language for the Ethereum blockchain called LaserCat. The main goal of LaserCat is to provide strong guarantees about the privacy of the data stored in a contract, while still allowing that data to be used in computations. LaserCat is based on the λ-π calculus, a variant of the simply typed λ-calculus with support for private names.
